RIYADH

Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia, is a city of beauty and contrasts. The city is home to a rich history and culture, dating back to the 18th century. Riyadh is also a major center of commerce and industry, and is home to a growing population of young people. The city's beauty is evident in its many parks and gardens, as well as its modern architecture. Riyadh is also home to a number of historical sites, including the Masmak Fortress, which was once the seat of the Saudi royal family. Riyadh is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city that is constantly evolving. The city is a must-see for anyone interested in Saudi Arabia's history, culture, and future.
